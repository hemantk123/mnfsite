Welcome to MyNextFilm.com

MyNextFilm is a project to bring together creative people from every part of the Film making process. Actors, Writers, Directors, Producers, Camera persons, Musicians, Singers……

Video or Film is the strongest expression tool we have, with the rise of the internet and technology video creation is expanding exponentially. Once you set foot towards creating your own video content, you seek to find the right people to work with, this is where it gets complicated. Neither is it easy to find people nor is it easy to find work, adding on to it there is a plethora of equipment required.

With MyNextFilm, we are committed to bridging this gap and assist in every way possible to make Film making easy.

Happy Filming!
